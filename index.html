Dragalia Lost Favourite Character Picker

HOW TO USE: It's simple! In each group of characters shown, click on at least one (or more) of your favourites from that group, then hit Pick! Hover over a character to see their name. You can also pass if you can't decide, or Undo if you made a mistake. Your favourites will appear on the right side!

Features all playable characters and story NPCs (including spoliers up to chapter 18). I will try to update this as new characters are added. If you notice any characters are missing or have any other suggestions, please let me know! (@Dragonite13#0149 on Discord or @Dragonite131 on Twitter!)

Based off the Cave of Dragonfly's Favourite Pokemon Picker found here: https://www.dragonflycave.com/favorite.html . Character images are WIP I'm making versions specifically for this and I'm lazy lol.
