Dragalia Lost Favourite Character Picker

HOW TO USE: It's simple! In each group of characters shown, click on at least one (or more) of your favourites from that group, then hit Pick! You can hover over a character to see their name. You can also pass if you can't decide, or Undo if you made a mistake. Your favourites will appear on the right side!

This features all playable characters and story NPCs (including spoliers up to chapter 18). I will try to update this as new characters are added. If you notice any characters are missing or have any other suggestions, please let me know! I'm also available if there's any other reason you want/need to contact me.
